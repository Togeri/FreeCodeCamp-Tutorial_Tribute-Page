Elon Musk

The real life Tony Stark

Elon Musk, founder of Tesla Motors, SpaceX and SolarCity

Elon Musk's achievements:

  • 1971 - Born in Pretoria, South Africa.
  • 1988 - Went to US. As he said: "That's where great things are possible".
  • 1955 - Founded Zip2.
  • 1999 - Sold Zip2 to Compaq Computer for $300 million.
  • 1999 - Founded X.com, that later on would be known as PayPal
  • 2002 - Elon sold PayPal to eBay for $1,150 million, with $150 million net profit.
  • 2002 - Founded SpaceX with the goal of achieving interplanetary existence.
  • 2003 - Founded Tesla Motors, world's first company producing 100% electric cars.
  • 2006 - Founded SolarCity, to provide citizens with affordable solar energized electric services.
  • 2010 - Invested in Halcyon Molecular, a company which prevents diseases working genetically.
  • 2010 - Founded OpenAI, an Open Source company aiming to develop an AI capable of governing human society for total welfare.
